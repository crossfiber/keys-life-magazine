Frequently Asked Questions

What is Keys Life Magazine's circulation reach?
Keys Life Magazine reaches 26,000 homes monthly across the Florida Keys from Ocean Reef to Long Key. We are the only direct mail publication that delivers to every mailbox in this region.
How much does advertising in Keys Life Magazine cost?
Advertising rates vary based on ad size and placement. We offer flexible options from small classified ads to full-page features. Contact our sales team at 305-453-6674 for detailed pricing and package information.
What are the advertising deadlines?
Content deadlines typically fall 3-4 weeks before the publication date. We recommend submitting materials early to ensure quality production. Contact our team for specific deadline dates for your desired issue.
Can I advertise online as well as in print?
Yes! We offer integrated advertising solutions combining print, digital, social media, and website marketing. Our team can create a comprehensive advertising strategy tailored to your business goals.
How do I submit a story or article?
We welcome story submissions from Keys community members. Please contact our editorial team at sales@keyslifemagazine.com with your story idea, photos, and relevant details. Our team will review and provide feedback.
Is there an online version of Keys Life Magazine?
Yes, current and past issues are available online. Visit our website to read the latest issue or browse our archives. Digital subscriptions are also available for those who prefer online reading.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and checks. For advertising invoices, we offer flexible payment terms and can set up recurring billing for regular clients.
How can I track my advertising results?
We recommend using unique coupon codes or dedicated phone numbers in your ads to track response. We can also help integrate tracking parameters in digital campaigns for comprehensive ROI measurement.
